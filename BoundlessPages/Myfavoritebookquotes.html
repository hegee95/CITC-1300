My Favorite Quotes

Here are some of my favorite quotes from the books I love. They have inspired me, made me think, and have been a great source of comfort when I needed it. Each quote holds a special place in my heart, reminding me of the wisdom and beauty found within the pages of these books. They serve as a beacon of hope during challenging times and a source of motivation when I need a little push. These words have the power to transport me back to the moments when I first read them, evoking the same emotions and reflections. I hope they resonate with you as much as they have with me, offering insight and solace whenever you need it.

These quotes have been with me through thick and thin, and I hope they will be with me for years to come. I hope that you, too, will enjoy them as much as I have.

Quotes from Throne of Glass

"You could rattle the stars," she whispered. "You could do anything, if only you dared. And deep down, you know it, too. That’s what scares you most." – Aelin Galathynius: Throne of Glass
